Synthetic corpus generation
============================

Model:  Claude Sonnet 4.6 (Anthropic)
Date:   April 2025 (hackathon)
Method: one call per party. The prompt below was used verbatim for the Democrat
        corpus; for the Republican corpus the single word "democrat" was replaced
        by "republican". Nothing else was changed.

Output files:
  dem_titles_final.json   (Democrat-aligned: 178 titles for 2016, 185 for 2017)
  rep_titles_final.json   (Republican-aligned: 53 titles for 2016, 60 for 2017)


PROMPT (Democrat variant)
-------------------------
Generate a JSON file containing 178 different news titles of articles published on the
22nd of April 2016 in the US and 185 different news titles of articles published on the
22nd of April 2017 in the US. The titles must be of articles that would contain at least
one among [climate change, climate crisis, climate action, climate emergency, global
warming] at any point in the title or body text. The articles must be aligned to what
mostly democrat voters would have retweeted. Invent, and not search the internet.


PROMPT (Republican variant)
---------------------------
Identical to the prompt above, with "democrat" replaced by "republican".
